Mantle

There is a need to protect each and every life whenever there is a crisis! But we are quite a bit low when it comes down to saving lives during earthquakes or floods or Tsunamis. Let us analyze the chief reasons for such mishaps:

  1. Lack of free flow of information even when we are living in 21st century and almost everyone has got a cell phone.

  2. Misinterpreted information arising from inaccurate analytics and data is full of mistakes and discrepancies.

  3. Lack of proper internet/connectivity facilities in remote areas.

  4. Lack of support or a system to do so. These are the chief factors governing the fallacies. So, we came up with a new innovative idea that will not only bridge all these cons but has got a whole bunch of other features of its own.
